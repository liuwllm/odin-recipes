Bolognese Sauce

Description

A recipe to make a lot of bolognese sauce all at once for quick and easy pasta.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 large red onion
  • 1 lb chicken livers
  • 3 lbs ground meat
  • 6 oz can tomato paste
  • 3 28 oz cans crushed tomatoes
  • 1 bottle white wine
  • Liquid chicken bouillon (or stock cube)
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp dried parsley
  • 1 tbsp dried thyme
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel and finely chop carrots and onions.
  2. In a large pot, fry vegetables in olive oil on high heat until soft, stirring constantly.
  3. Dump vegetables out into bowl or plate.
  4. Finely chop livers until almost pureed.
  5. In big pot, fry ground meat in olive oil on high heat, stirring and scraping constantly with wooden spoon to keep meatballs from forming.
  6. Cook until most of the water evaporates and pan starts to crackle.
  7. Add livers and tomato paste and stir to combine.
  8. Pour in wine and scrape when brown residue on bottom of pot appears to burn.
  9. Stir in bouillon.
  10. Reduce heat to simmer and cook for two hours, stirring occasionally.
  11. After two hours, season sauce with herbs and vinegar.
  12. Simmer an additional hour.
  13. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper once desired thickness is reached.
  14. Use or freeze in ice cube trays for future use.

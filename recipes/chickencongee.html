Chicken Congee

Description

A simple congee (rice porridge) dish with chicken and a green onion garnish.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 12 oz chicken
  • 8 cup water
  • 0.50 oz ginger
  • 2 pieces green onion
  • Cilantro
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp chicken bouillon

Chicken Marinade

  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 0.50 tsp chicken bouillon
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Wash rice and drain 3 times.
  2. Boil 8 cups of water and chop ginger, cilantro, and green onion into small pieces.
  3. Cut chicken into thin slices and place in a bowl.
  4. Add oyster sauce, cornstarch, chicken bouillon, and water into the bowl.
  5. Mix and massage the chicken and marinade together until no liquid remains.
  6. Add vegetable oil to the bowl and mix around for 20-30 seconds.
  7. Add rice to the boiling water and partially cover pot to cook for 25 minutes on medium heat.
  8. Whisk the rice.
  9. Set the stove to high heat and slowly add chicken over the course of a minute.
  10. Add ginger, salt, and chicken bouillon.
  11. Plate and add remaining green onion and cilantro garnish.

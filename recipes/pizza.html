Oven Pizza

Description

A recipe that allows you to make pizza at home using an oven.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 1/4 cups warm water
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp active dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt
  • 5 cups bread flour
  • Additional oil and flour to work and grease the dough
  • Cornmeal, semolina flour, or coarse-ground whole wheat flour for dusting

Sauce

  • 1 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2-4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dried oregano

Cheese

  • 24-32 oz whole-milk, low-moisture mozzarella, freshly grated (6-8 oz per pizza)
  • Grated parmesan for dusting

Steps

  1. Combine water, sugar, yeast in large bowl and let sit for a few minutes.
  2. Add olive oil, salt, and 5 cups of bread flour.
  3. Mix until combined, then start kneading.
  4. Add enough additional flour to keep dough workable and kneed until the dough can be stretched into a thin sheet without tearing.
  5. Divide dough into four equal balls and put them in four containers.
  6. Lightly coat balls and interior of containers with olive oil.
  7. Cover and rise at room temperature for two hours or put them in the fridge to rise for 1-7 days.
  8. Once done rising, put pizza stone or steel into oven and preheat to highest possible temperature for a full hour.
  9. Mix the sauce ingredients together.
  10. Dust a pizza peel with cornmeal or a similar substitute.
  11. Take the dough out and stretch to widest size that will fit on pizza peel.
  12. Top with sauce to lightly coat the surface, then dust with parmesan and cover with mozzarella.
  13. Transfer pizza to stone/steel and bake until crust is well-browned and cheese has browned a bit.
